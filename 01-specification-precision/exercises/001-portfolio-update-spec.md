# Project Spec: Portfolio Update
Date: 2026-04-01

## What is this project?
A freelance portfolio for Jamie O'Beirne (CodeCraft) targeting
artists in Barcelona and potentially elsewhere in Spain and 
Catalonia who need affordable, hand-built websites.

## Who is the audience?
Artists, musicians, painters, writers and photographers based 
in Barcelona and Catalonia who want a web presence but don't 
want to use Wordpress or Squarespace. They value creativity, 
authenticity, and personal connection over corporate polish.

## What is the goal?
Within 10 seconds a visitor should understand who Jamie is, 
what he builds, and feel confident enough to get in touch. 
They should see real work, read real testimonials, and feel 
like Jamie understands the creative world they live in.

## What must be included?
- Original homepage with clear value proposition
- Portfolio of real completed projects with live links
- Genuine testimonials
- Clear and easy navigation
- Personal bio that reflects Jamie's real background
- Mobile responsiveness across all screen sizes
- All graphic elements functional and visually strong
- Contact method

## What must NOT be included?
- Clutter or unnecessary decoration
- Placeholder or fake content
- Generic corporate language

## Tone & voice
Creative, warm, friendly. Speaks to artists as a fellow 
creative — not as a tech salesperson.

## Success criteria
- A first time visitor understands what Jamie does in 10 seconds
- Users feel excited about the portfolio and tech skills on display
- Testimonials create genuine credibility and trust
- Site looks good and functions on all screen sizes
- All graphic elements look good and function correctly
- All portfolio links go to real live projects
- Navigation is clear and easy to use
- Spanish is the default language on page load

## Constraints
- Vanilla JS, HTML, CSS (no frameworks)
- Zero cost deployment (GitHub Pages)
- No monthly fees or CMS

## Language
Spanish default on page load. English and Catalan available 
via toggle. (Existing Eng/Es/Cat switcher to be updated to 
default to Spanish)
